## Patterns of the Top 10%

Every elite Oklahoma roofing site does these 5 things. Average sites skip at least 3.

### 1. Named, specific testimonials
Every top site shows real customer names (not "Happy Customer"). Some show faces. The testimonials mention specific outcomes: "My claim was denied and they got it reversed."

### 2. A clear step-by-step claims process
Top sites walk visitors through exactly what happens — typically 4 steps. This reduces fear and positions the company as guides, not just contractors.

### 3. Certifications and trust badges above the fold
GAF, CertainTeed, BBB A+, HAAG Certified — these appear in the nav or hero. Average sites bury them in the footer or omit them.

### 4. Owner/team personality
The best local sites name the owner(s) prominently. Wing Brothers names Zack and Dusty. This works powerfully in small-town Oklahoma markets where people want to know *who* they're dealing with.

### 5. Specific material brands mentioned
"We use CertainTeed shingles and James Hardie siding" — this signals quality and lets homeowners research. Average sites say "quality materials" and stop there.
